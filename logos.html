Usage Hierarchy

70% Black & White
20% Kiwi Green
7% Founder Gold
3% Steel

Kiwi Green — The Operator

Used for primary CTAs, logo accents, active states, brand signatures. This is the color people associate with Vektor. It says: the AI is here, and it's working.

Founder Gold — The Visionary

Reserved for founder-related contexts, premium moments, partnership marks, and special editions. Sparingly used to maintain its weight and significance.

Steel — The Structure

Secondary text, borders, metadata, subtle UI elements. Steel provides the scaffolding without competing for attention. Functional, never decorative.
